The order of the units of computer storage from smallest to largest is: 
Bit: The smallest unit, which is one-eighth of a byte 
Byte: Eight bits make up one byte 
Kilobyte: One thousand bytes, or 1,000 bytes 
Megabyte: One million bytes, or 1,000,000 bytes 
Gigabyte: One billion bytes, or 1,000,000,000 bytes 
Terabyte: One trillion bytes, or 1,000,000,000,000 bytes
